Blair Erickson is a technology expert and creative director whose work spans entertainment fields from film, television, video games, interactive virtual worlds, to online video experiences. He wrote and directed the Netflix released thriller "Banshee Chapter," the first feature film in history released in a virtual reality format. Most recently completed production of Cisco's virtual city project and the Siggraph 2016 special presentation of "Sagea" a decentralized virtual blockchain powered media community.

As the Creative Director of the interactive agency Millions of Us he created interactive experiences and live-action narratives for clients like Google, Fox, Warner Bros, DreamWorks, and Playstation in virtual worlds and social communities. He served as a creative director on the virtual communities of Playstation Home and Google Rooms. His campaign for WWE nominated for a Webby and won the LACP Spotlight award for a first-of-its-kind campaign bringing a World Wrestling Entertainment invasion into the world Gaia Online. It's still written about as a business case study in books like "Changing the Game."


Tech specialties: Javascript, Node.JS, Unity, Unreal 4.0, Interact.JS, react-native, PHP, firebase, mySQL, PixiJS, JQuery, HTML5, virtual reality interfaces and blockchain technology.
